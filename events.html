WHAT WE PARTICIPATE IN

TitleTown Events is proud to facilitate and coordinate collegiate sporting events in Hawaii. Including smaller collegiate sporting teams: Division II, Division III, NAIA, and NCCAA. Currently, we offer events in three sports: basketball, softball, and soccer.

Our three major events we currently particpate in are the Big Island Classic (basketball), the Ulili Invitational (softball), and a spring break soccer event. Along with facilitating these events, we also streamline travel services for the team and work with individual companies to to aid in other parts of travel.

BIG ISLAND CLASSIC

Basketball - D2, D3, NAIA, NCCAA

Ran in Hilo, Hawaii.

The Big Island Classic event is currently held both the Friday and Saturday after Thanksgiving and the week before Christmas.

ULILI SOFTBALL INVITATIONAL

Softball - D2, D3, NAIA

Ran in Honolulu, Hawaii.

The Ulili Softball Invitational is currently held during the first two weeks in February and March.

SOCCER EVENT

Soccer - D1, D2, D3, NAIA, NCCAA

Ran in Hilo, Hawaii.

The soccer event is currrently held during universities' spring breaks.
