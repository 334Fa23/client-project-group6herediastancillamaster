Game Facilities

Saint Louis Field - Chaminade

The Saint Louis Field is a soccer field located in Honolulu, Hawaii. It's a well-known facility that offers a greatplaying surface for soccer. Not only does it feature a beautiful pitch that makes playing a real pleasure, but it alsoboasts beautiful views of Kalaepohaku Ridge. The field is a favorite among soccer players thanks to its convenientlocation and excellent upkeep. Whether you're a competitive soccer player or just looking for a fun recreationalactivity, Saint Louis Field is the perfect place for a soccer frenzy to occur.

Waipi'o Soccer Complex - 18U Club Team

Waipio soccer complex is a state-of-the-art sports and recreation facility located in Waipahu, Hawaii. It featuresseveral grass and artificial turf fields for soccer, rugby, and football. The complex also has a fitness center, ameeting room, and a concession stand. Waipio soccer complex has hosted numerous local and international eventsand tournaments, including the 2005 Little League World Series and multiple soccer championships. It is a populardestination for athletes and sports enthusiasts, offering top-notch amenities and facilities for all levels ofcompetition.

Atherton Field - Hawaii Pacific

The Atherton soccer field in Hawaii is a pristine green space perfect for sports enthusiasts. Located in ManoaValley, it provides a picturesque backdrop for a game of soccer. The field is well-maintained and equipped withamenities such as benches, restrooms, and drinking fountains. The Atherton soccer field is popular with locals andvisitors alike, and is a great place to spend a sunny afternoon with friends and family. Surrounded by lush tropicalfoliage, it offers a peaceful respite from the hustle and bustle of city life.

UH Soccer Grassfield - University of Hawaii

The Hawaii soccer grass field is a popular spot for players and fans alike. The lush green grass provides a perfectplaying surface for teams of all ages and skill levels. The field is well-maintained and offers plenty of space forplayers to run and maneuver. Spectators can enjoy the action from the sidelines, and there are plenty of amenitiesnearby, including restrooms and parking. Whether you're looking for a casual pickup game or a competitivematch, the Hawaii soccer grass field is a great choice.
