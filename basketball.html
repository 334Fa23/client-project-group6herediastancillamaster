BIG ISLAND CLASSIC

The Big Island Classic events takes place in Hilo, Hawaii, over two seperate occasions. Typically, the event is held both the Friday and Saturday after Thanksgiving and the week before Christmas.

GAMES, LODGING, GROUND TRANSPORTATION, AND BASE ACTIVITIES INCLUDED!

GAME FACILITY

AFOOK-CHINEN CIVIC AUDITORIUM

The Afook-Chinen Civic Auditorium — originally named Hilo Civic Auditorium — was built to promote sports andother cultural events. It was the first building completed in 1957 on what would become the Ho‘olulu ParkComplex. Afook-Chinen Civic Auditorium serves as the home for the Vulcans men's and women's basketball teams.

Ho‘olulu Park Complex is owned and operated by the County of Hawai‘i in Hilo and is a five-minute drive from theUniversity of Hawai‘i at Hilo campus.
