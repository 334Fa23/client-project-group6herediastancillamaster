LODGING

Courtyard by Marriott Waikiki Beach

Located just steps from Waikiki Beach, the Courtyard by Marriott Waikiki Beach offers contemporary accommodations with modern amenities. Guests can enjoy the hotel's outdoor pool, fitness center, and on-site restaurant serving American cuisine. The rooms and suites feature plush bedding, flat-screen TVs, and free Wi-Fi.Many also offer ocean or city views. The hotel is within walking distance of popular shopping, dining, and entertainment options in Waikiki. For those looking to explore further, the hotel is a short drive from popular attractions such as Diamond Head and Pearl Harbor.

Ohana East, Outrigger Resorts

The Ohana East is a comfortable and stylish hotel located just steps away from the famous Waikiki Beach inHonolulu, Hawaii. The hotel offers a variety of accommodations, including rooms and suites with kitchenettes. Guests can enjoy two outdoor swimming pools, a hot tub, a sauna, and a fitness center. The hotel also features arestaurant, bar, and coffee shop on site. The location is ideal for those who want to explore the area, with countless restaurants, shops, and attractions nearby. The friendly staff and excellent amenities make the Ohana East a great choice for a Hawaiian vacation.

Hilo Hawaiian Hotel

The Hilo Hawaiian Hotel is a beautiful beach front property located in Hilo, Hawaii. The hotel offers a variety of accommodations from standard rooms to suites, all of which feature private lanais with views of either the oceanor the lush tropical gardens. On-site amenities include a restaurant, bar, outdoor pool, fitness center, and beach access. The hotel is ideally situated for exploring nearby attractions such as Hawaii Volcanoes National Park, Rainbow Falls, and Hilo Farmers Market. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, the Hilo Hawaiian Hotelprovides a comfortable and convenient stay.

GROUND TRANSPORTATION

All ground transportation is round trip affairs.

O'ahu Transportation - Hawaii Super Transit

On O'ahu, ground transportation is done through Hawaii Super Transit. They offer a variety of charter bus services for group transportation needs. They will provide the group with airport transfers, and shuttle services from games, practices, and activities. Wilbert and his staff are top notch! He provides your team with a comfortable ride in a well air-conditioned motor coach.

Their services are only included on Oahu.

Hilo Transportation - TitleTown Staff & Outside Company

On Hilo, ground transportation is done by TitleTown Staff and an outside company.
