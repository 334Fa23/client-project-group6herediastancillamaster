GAME FACILITY

MAULIOLA SPORT COMPLEX AT SAND ISLAND STATE PARK

Mauliola Sport Complex is located in Honolulu, Hawaii. Sand Island State Park was once home to a World War II prisoner of war camp. Remnants from World War II can still be found, like the guard towers, bunkers, and a firing range. The complex offers multiple playing fields and other accommodations.

PROJECT TIMELINE

1

Event Set Up

Once we confirm with the Hawai'i Universities and tournament directors about events we start to create flyers and advertisements to get colleges interested in making these events happen!

2

Initial Contact

In this stage we will begin our relationship as we get to know one another and learn more about your teams' needs while on the Island.

3

Travel Package

Once you have reviewed the information from the initial contact and have decided to participate in this event, it is time to choose one of our three travel packages.

4

Contract Signed

Once we confirm with you on the travel package we will then get a contract sent up. An initial deposit is due 30 days after signed contract.

Then mark your calendars and look forward to experienceing the great state of Hawai'i.

5

Land in Hawai'i

Transportation will pick you up at the airport and will take you to the hotel. From there your experience is just beginning. Your games, practices, and activities will be awaiting you in the upcoming days.

6

Travel Home

Unfortunately, your time in paradise is over. We look forward to seeing you come back in the future!
