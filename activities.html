ACTIVITIES

DIAMOND HEAD HIKE

Diamond Head is a volcanic cone on the Hawaiian island of Oahu that is known for its stunning panoramic views. It is a popular tourist destination and hiking spot, with a steep trail leading to the summit that provides breathtaking views of Waikiki and the Pacific Ocean. The cone was formed over 300,000 years ago and is made of tuff, a type of volcanic ash that has solidified. Diamond Head is named as such because 19th-century British sailors believed they had discovered diamonds on the beachat its base.

Includes complimentary scones from Diamond Head Market. Diamond Head Market in Honolulu is famous forits mouth-watering scones.

MAKAPU'U LIGHTHOUSE TRAIL

The Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail is a picturesque hike located on the south eastern tip of Oahu in Hawaii.The trail is approximately two miles round-trip and offers stunning views of the ocean and surrounding islands. The highlight of the trail is the Makapu'u Lighthouse, which stands on a scenic cliff 600 feet above sea level and has been guiding ships since 1909. The trail is easy to moderate and suitable for all skill levels, making it a popular destination for families. Additionally, hikers may spot whales and seabirds along the way.

ARIZONA MEMORIAL TOUR

The Arizona Memorial Tour is a unique experience that takes visitors to the USS Arizona Memorial in Pearl Harbor, Hawaii. The memorial commemorates the lives lost during the attack on Pearl Harbor on December 7, 1941. Visitors begin at the Pearl Harbor Visitor Center, where they can view exhibits and watch a documentary about the attack. They then board a boat to the memorial, which is built over the sunken remains of the USS Arizona. Visitors can pay their respects to the 1,177 sailors and marines who lost their lives and reflect on the impact of the attack on World War II.

There are several free museums and exhibits related to Pearl Harbor, Hawaii. These include the Pearl Harbor Visitor Center and the Pacific Aviation Museum Pearl Harbor.

We can set up group tours at an additional rate that is not already included in our travel packages.

PARADISE COVE LUAU

The Paradise Cove Luau is an unforgettable experience located in Oahu, Hawaii. As guests arrive, they can participate in traditional Hawaiian activities, such as flower lei-making or hula dancing. The evening then continues with a stunning sunset ceremony, followed by a delicious buffet dinner featuring traditional Hawaiian cuisine. The night concludes with an exciting Polynesian cultural show, featuring stunning fire knife dancers and live music and dance performances. The Paradise Cove Luau is a must-see attraction for anyone visiting Hawaiiand is a perfect way to immerse yourself in the rich culture and traditions of the islands.
